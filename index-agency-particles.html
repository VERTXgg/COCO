About us

We Currently Host A Earth Smap

Welcome to our Minecraft server, where the world is truly at your fingertips! Our server features a detailed map of the Earth, allowing you to explore, build, and survive in a blocky rendition of the planet we all call home. Whether you're looking to recreate famous landmarks, establish your own empire, or simply wander the continents, our Earth map offers endless possibilities.

Lovely

Custom Terrain Generation

While the map is based on Earth, we’ve added custom terrain features to make exploration even more exciting. Discover hidden caves, massive mountain ranges, and unique biomes that blend realism with creativity.

Advanced Economy System

Our server features a sophisticated economy where players can trade resources, establish businesses, and manage currencies. Engage in global trade, stock markets, or even become a tycoon in your own nation.
